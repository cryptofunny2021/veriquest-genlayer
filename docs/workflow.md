Campaign creator creates a quest.

---

## Step 2

Participants submit content.

---

## Step 3

AI evaluates content.

- Score
- Reason

---

Gold:
80-100

Silver:
40-79

Rejected:
0-39

## Step 5

Reputation system updates contributor profile.

---

## Step 6

Decision engine selects winner.

---

Campaign status becomes:

- Approved
- Review
- Rejected
