# VeriQuest Architecture

VeriQuest consists of five major layers.

## Quest Layer

Responsible for campaign creation and configuration.

## Evaluation Layer

Uses GenLayer AI consensus to evaluate subjective content.

## Reward Layer

Assigns reward tiers based on evaluation scores.

Reward Tiers:

* Gold
* Silver
* Rejected

## Reputation Layer

Tracks:

* Reputation Score
* Successful Submissions
* Failed Submissions
* Reward History

## Decision Layer

Generates final campaign outcomes.

Possible outcomes:

* Approved
* Review
* Rejected

---

Workflow:

Quest Creation

↓

Content Submission

↓

AI Evaluation

↓

Score Assignment

↓

Reward Classification

↓

Reputation Update

↓

Campaign Decision

↓

Winner Selection
