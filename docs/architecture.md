# VeriQuest Architecture

VeriQuest is built around four interconnected modules.

## Quest Engine

Responsible for:

* Campaign creation
* Metadata storage
* Category management

## AI Evaluation Engine

Responsible for:

* Content scoring
* AI consensus evaluation
* Quality assessment

## Reputation Engine

Responsible for:

* Reputation score tracking
* Success history
* Contributor performance

## Decision Engine

Responsible for:

* Winner selection
* Campaign approval
* Campaign review
* Campaign rejection

## Data Flow

Quest Creation

↓

Content Submission

↓

AI Evaluation

↓

Reward Assignment

↓

Reputation Update

↓

Campaign Decision
